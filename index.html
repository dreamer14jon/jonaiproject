Group Marketing Squad Design

Interactive agent-based workflow for a MacBook campaign, coordinated by an Orchestrator Agent with only two key human checkpoints.

Central Coordinator

Orchestrator Agent

Keeps the human team posted on what is happening across the workflow. It tracks progress, routes outputs between agents, highlights issues, and triggers human review only at the two critical checkpoints.

👁️

Perceive

Receives campaign inputs, agent outputs, timeline, progress updates and issues.

🧭

Plan

Decides task order, readiness criteria and priority routing.

🔀

Act

Assigns work, passes outputs forward and sends issues back.

📊

Observe

Monitors progress, quality, issues and when human review is needed.

When to send back to Research

Competitor data is missing, pricing looks outdated, market reaction is unclear, or audience insight is weak.

When to send back to Briefing

The campaign focus is unclear, the benefit does not connect to the brand attribute, or the platform choice is not convincing.

When to send back to Content

The message is off-brand, too generic, not platform-fit, or needs clearer copy before compliance or approval.

Key Principles

  • Humans review only at two critical points.
  • Agents handle heavy lifting, humans provide judgement.
  • The Orchestrator keeps humans posted and checks connection between steps.
  • Every agent must produce a usable output for the next agent.
